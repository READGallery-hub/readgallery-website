讀畫樓介紹

读画楼始建于宋代，历经千年沧桑，承载着深厚的文化底蕴。这里不仅是艺术品的收藏之所，更是文人雅士品茶论道、探讨人生哲理的雅集之地。

我们致力于传承和弘扬中华优秀传统文化，通过精心挑选的古代艺术品，为现代人提供一个感受东方美学、体验文化魅力的精神家园。每一件藏品都承载着历史的记忆，诉说着古人的智慧与情怀。

在这里，您可以静心品味每一幅画作的笔墨韵味，感受每一件器物的工艺之美，聆听每一段历史的回响。读画楼，不仅是一座艺术画廊，更是一座连接古今的文化桥梁。

Read Gallery traces its origins to the Song dynasty. Through a thousand years of change, it carries a profound cultural heritage. More than a place of collection, it is a gathering ground for scholars and connoisseurs — for tea, discourse, and the contemplation of life's deeper truths.

We are devoted to preserving and advancing the finest traditions of Chinese culture. Through carefully selected ancient works of art, we offer a spiritual home where modern visitors may experience the beauty of Eastern aesthetics. Each piece carries the memory of history and speaks to the wisdom and sensibility of the ancients.

Here you may quietly savor the brushwork of each painting, feel the beauty of craft in each object, and listen to the echoes of history. Read Gallery is not merely an art gallery — it is a cultural bridge connecting past and present.

" 画者，心印也。笔墨之间，见性情之真；丹青之上，显精神之妙。 " — 宋代画论
" Painting is the seal of the heart. Between brush and ink, one sees the truth of temperament; upon color and form, the wonder of the spirit is revealed. " — Song Dynasty Treatise on Painting

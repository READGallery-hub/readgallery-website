直颈，唇口、平底，颈腹有两只扁平竖耳，繪有黑彩璇涡纹，线条流畅，似水奔流不息。彩陶是古陶器中最具大美的艺术品。 杭州讀畫樓藏

Straight neck with lipped rim and flat base. Two flat vertical lug handles between neck and belly. Painted with black swirl patterns in fluid, flowing lines evoking ceaseless water. Painted pottery represents the grandest aesthetic achievement among ancient ceramics. Collection of Read Gallery (杭州讀畫樓)

《骏马图》
传统中国画 · 水墨纸本 · 立轴

此画为传统中国水墨画，描绘一匹骏马在山水间悠然自得。画中马匹神态生动，笔法流畅，体现了中国画"以形写神"的艺术特色。 中国画注重"神韵"的表达，此作品通过简洁的笔触和巧妙的构图，生动地展现了骏马的神态和山水的气势，体现了中国画"天人合一"的哲学思想。

A traditional Chinese ink painting depicting a spirited horse at ease amidst a landscape. The horse is rendered with vivid expression and fluid brushwork, embodying the principle of "capturing the spirit through form." Through economical brushstrokes and masterful composition, this work conveys the horse's spirit and the grandeur of the landscape, reflecting the philosophical ideal of harmony between humanity and nature.

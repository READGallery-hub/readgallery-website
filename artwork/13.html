收藏故事

此杯得于河南漯河古玩市场。在旧货摊间搜寻三小时之久，终于在一只旧箱中发现了这件半隐的青釉小杯。漯河地处中原腹地，历来是古玩流通的重要集散地，许多唐宋窑口的精品曾在此地民间流转。牛首杯器形小巧精致，青釉温润如玉，为耀州窑唐代产品中的少见器型。

Provenance Story

Found at the Luohe antiques market in Henan province after three hours of searching. The small celadon cup was discovered half-hidden in a box at a secondhand goods stall. Luohe, situated in the heartland of central China, has long been an important hub for the circulation of antiques, where fine pieces from Tang and Song kilns have passed through private hands for generations. This ox-head cup is a rare form among Tang-dynasty Yaozhou kiln products, with a compact, refined shape and warm jade-like celadon glaze.

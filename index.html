讀畫樓介紹

杭州讀畫樓，程氏家族私人收藏。讀畫樓之名，取「讀畫」之意——以文人之心，品鑒歷代丹青與器物之妙。數十年來，程氏一門秉持對中國古代藝術的熱忱，精心匯聚了一批跨越新石器時代至清代的珍貴藏品。

收藏涵蓋陶瓷、玉器、青銅、佛教造像及書畫諸門類，尤以歷代名窯陶瓷與高古玉器見長。自馬家窯彩陶之樸拙，至宋代官窯之清雅，再到明清文房之精巧，每一件藏品皆承載着中華文明綿延五千年的審美精神。

讀畫樓崇尚中國文人精神和生活情趣，願以古代藝術之美，連接古今，啟迪心靈。杭州、香港、西雅圖——三地珍藏，一脈相承。

Hangzhou Read Gallery (讀畫樓) is the private collection of the Cheng family. The name "Read Gallery" reflects its founding philosophy: to approach art with the contemplative eye of the Chinese literati — reading paintings and objects as one reads poetry.

Over decades, the collection has grown to encompass ceramics, jade, bronze, Buddhist sculpture, and calligraphy spanning from the Neolithic period to the Qing dynasty. Particular strengths include kiln ceramics from across China's great dynasties and archaic jade — from the primal beauty of Majiayao painted pottery to the refined elegance of Song dynasty wares.

Read Gallery is devoted to the spirit and refined taste of the Chinese literati tradition, aspiring to connect past and present through the enduring beauty of ancient art. The collection is housed across Hangzhou, Hong Kong, and Seattle — three cities, one unbroken tradition.
